Full Rulebook
Squad
Pick 5 active players + 2 reserves from a £75m budget. Minimum price £4.5m.
Captain and Vice Captain
Captain scores 2x points. Vice Captain scores 1.5x points.
Finish Positions
Major win +37 · Tournament win +25 · 2nd place +15 · Top 5 +10 · Top 10 +6 · Top 20 +3 · Missed cut -10 · LIV bottom 27 -10
Stroke Play
Hole in one +15 · Eagle +8 · Birdie +3 · Par 0 · Bogey -1 · Double -3 · Triple -5 · Blob -8
Multipliers
Signature Events: 1.25x finish multiplier · Majors: 1.5x finish multiplier
Major Bonus
Own a player all 4 major weeks: 2 majors +75 · 3 majors +200 · All 4 +500
Transfers
1 free transfer per week. Unused transfers carry over — max 2 banked. Extra transfers cost -4 points each.
Chips — once per season each
Wildcard: unlimited free transfers for one gameweek. Triple Captain: captain scores 3x for one gameweek. Bench Boost: all 7 players score including reserves for one gameweek.
Leagues
Global league automatic. Free private leagues with invite codes. Paid leagues with pot to winner — coming soon.
How To Play
Step 1 — Build Your Team
Go to Transfers and pick 7 players within your £75m budget. Think about the week ahead — who is playing, and in what type of event? Majors and Signature Events score more.
Step 2 — Set Captain and Vice Captain
In My Squad, your captain sits on the green and scores double. Your vice captain scores 1.5x. Pick your best player for the week.
Step 3 — Watch Live
Head to Live Scores during tournament weekend. Star players to follow them at the top every week.
Step 4 — Make Transfers
After each gameweek you get 1 free transfer. Unused transfers bank to a max of 2. Use Wildcard for a full squad overhaul — once per season.
Using Chips Wisely
Triple Captain: use in a Major week when your captain is in form. Bench Boost: use when all 7 players are playing and in form. Wildcard: use when your squad needs a full reset.
Leagues
You are automatically in the global league. Create private leagues for friends using a custom code. Highest season total wins.
